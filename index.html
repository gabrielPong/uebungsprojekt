About APXGP

APXGP is an independent Formula One team built on one idea: we don’t wait for the future — we engineer it. We combine race-proven experience with next-generation talent to create a car that can fight anywhere: on high-speed straights, in dirty air, under pressure, in the final laps when it actually matters.

Our philosophy is simple. Precision over ego. Data over noise. Execution over talk. Every lap is treated like a statement: controlled aggression, zero hesitation, maximum commitment. From power unit calibration to tyre life management, we operate with one goal — extract 100% pace when it counts.

APXGP is led on track by Sonny Hayes, a veteran with elite race craft, late-braking instinct, and ridiculous one-lap discipline — and Joshua Pearce, raw pace, relentless qualifying speed, and no fear in wheel-to-wheel fights. Together they form a driver lineup built to score, not just survive.

Off track, APXGP runs as a performance company, not a nostalgia brand. We build our own identity, our own culture, and our own future. No excuses. No comfort zones. Just results.
